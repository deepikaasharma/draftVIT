Fettucine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  1. Fettucine Pasta
  2. Alfredo Sauce
  3. Broccoli
  4. Spinach
  5. Black pepper
  6. Italian Seasoning
  7. Olive oil
  8. Salt

Cooking Instruction:

  • Step 1: Boil Fettucine Pasta Aldente. Drain. Mix with olive oil.Keep aside.
  • Step 2: Heat olive oil. Add Italian Seasoning and ground black pepper. Add chopped broccoli and spinach. Stir fry. Add Alfredo Sauce. Add Salt as needed.
  • Step 3: Add Pasta to the pan. Mix.

Serve and enjoy!
